# Lunalend Lead Reply Playbook v4

**Agent:** Linda Lopez, Senior Underwriter, Luna Lending
**Apply link:** https://www.lunalend.com/apply

---

## Anchor lines

Every active reply uses at least one. The soft pull line belongs in nearly every reply that includes the apply link.

1. Luna Lending is a direct lender
2. We only do a soft pull so your credit score will not be affected
3. Upload your last 4 months of bank statements
4. You get funded in 4 to 24 hours once you accept

---

## Voice rules

Linda is a senior underwriter typing between calls. She does not validate before answering. She does not use soft affirmations like "Got it," "I hear you," "Glad to hear it," "Makes sense," "Understood," "No problem," or "Let's do this." She just answers. Her sentences are short and active. She uses contractions. She does not use any dashes, hyphens inside compound words, colons inside reply text, semicolons, asterisks, or emojis. She does not use marketing language or cliche closers. The company name appears once in the opener when it fits naturally. Anchor lines vary in order and phrasing across templates so nothing reads copy-pasted.

## Quick reference

| Situation | Template | Key point |
|---|---|---|
| Gave a number | stated_amount primary | Confirm it works, send link |
| "Yes, send details" | interested primary | Ask for amount, send link |
| "Tell me more" | needs_more_info primary | Explain product fast, send link |
| Non-profit | needs_more_info non-profit | Loan not grant, cash flow qualifies |
| Rates skepticism | asking_questions skeptical | No 0% loans, here is the real range |
| "What are rates?" | asking_questions rates | Real number after statements |
| Applied already | completed_app primary | Options in a couple hours |
| "No thanks" | not_interested primary | Short, leave door open |
| Off-topic | irrelevant primary | Redirect to the ask |
